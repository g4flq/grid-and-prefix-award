The Worked All Canadian Provinces Collectors Sheet

To claim this VE Collectors sheet you need to confirm only one (1)
Province (not including VE0, VE8, CY0, CY9, VY0 and VY1), as per the
VE requirements in the Grid-locator Tables.
This fine certificate is for the Canadian Province and Territory collector.
If you complete VE1-VE7, VE9, VO1, VO2 and VY2 you get to claim the
DVE PINNACLE award on which there is space to collect the harder
Canadian Marine VE0, VE8, CY0, CY9, VY0 and VY1.
