The Ultimate VK Certificate, DVK. You supply the frame!

GAPA-DVK (digital VK) award.
If you complete all VK1 - VK8 you get to claim the DVK PINNACLE award on which there is space to collect the harder Australian stickers VK0h, VK0m, VK9c, l, m, n, w, x, and y territories.
These awards are numbered, and in different modes each being shown on the endorsement BAR or stamp. The achievement recognition will be posted in the relevant mode OLAR table.
