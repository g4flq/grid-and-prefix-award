The DX collectors Sheet
 "DX BAR expansion sheets"  are available

To claim this DX Collectors sheet you need to confirm only one (1)
DX Entity as per the DX requirements in the Grid-locator Tables

There are 26 BAR spaces per sheet. Just request another sheet when
you need one.
Don't forget to claim the special Antarctic and Marine Mobile BARS
that are available and count towards your DX totals.

(Digital DXCC Award)
