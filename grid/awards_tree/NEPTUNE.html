The NEPTUNE Award. (digital working Marine Mobile stations)
This very difficult award is offered to anyone confirming all 20 WET Collector Sheet Grid Squares.  
Wet Award endorsements are available in the following increments.  First 4 Stamps in increments of 5,
next 5 in increments of 10, then 5 in increments of 50, and the last 4 in increments of 100.  
The Stamps are matched to meld with the existing art work. 
These awards are offered in different modes each being shown on the endorsement BAR or stamp.
